2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name, surname, and email address. We also collect your password, which is immediately hashed using the BCrypt algorithm and is never stored in a readable format.
  • Profile Information: You may optionally provide additional information such as your phone number, date of birth, and gender.
  • User Content: We collect and store all data you generate through the use of the Service, including but not limited to activity names, durations, details, categories, costs, and locations ("Activity Data").

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Security and Usage Data: We automatically collect your last login timestamp and failed login attempts to protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • Device Information for Push Notifications: If you grant permission, we collect a unique, anonymous Push Notification Token for your device, provided by Google's Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), to send you push notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the core functionality of the Service.
  • To authenticate you, secure your account, and manage your user session.
  • To send you essential transactional communications, such as password reset emails.
  • To send push notifications related to your activities, if enabled.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with the following third-party service providers who are essential for the operation of our Service:

  • Google LLC (Firebase Cloud Messaging & SMTP): We use Google's services to deliver push notifications to your device and to send transactional emails. Your interaction with these features is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Password Hashing: We use the strong, adaptive BCrypt hashing algorithm to protect your password.
  • Data Encryption in Transit: All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS).
  • Authentication: User sessions are managed using digitally signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT) to ensure secure access to our API.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data and Activity Data for as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address provided in Section 1. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently erase your data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Your Rights under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights, including the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data without parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
